# AFIP Research Metrics & Leaderboard Guide

## Purpose
Read eligible Pack 5.8 outcomes and aggregate them by candidate or market attributes.

## Metrics
Sample count, win/loss/flat counts, win rate, net and average points, median, population standard deviation, downside deviation, profit factor, expectancy, holding bars/seconds, MFE, MAE, reward-to-adverse ratio, 95% confidence interval, Sharpe-like score, stability score and sample sufficiency.

## Ranking safeguards
Rows remain visible even when ineligible. Default minimum sample is 30. A row is excluded from research ranking when sample minimum is not met or the lower 95% confidence bound is non-positive. No automatic execution promotion exists.
