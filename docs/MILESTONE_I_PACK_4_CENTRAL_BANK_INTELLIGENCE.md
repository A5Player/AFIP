# Milestone I Pack 4 — Central Bank Intelligence

Adds deterministic classification of FOMC, ECB, BOE, BOJ, and PBOC policy statements and speeches. The runtime produces structured policy bias, estimated GOLD# context, confidence, bilingual explanations, and next review time. It never sends orders and keeps live execution disabled.

## Runtime input
Use `central_bank_observations` with institution, communication type, speaker, statement or explicit policy bias.

## Policy
XM only, GOLD# only, paper/demo/simulation only. News and central-bank communications are intelligence inputs, never execution instructions.
