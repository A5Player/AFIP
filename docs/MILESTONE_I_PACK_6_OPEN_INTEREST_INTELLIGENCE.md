# Milestone I Pack 6 — Open Interest Intelligence

Adds deterministic futures open-interest analysis for XM GOLD#. The runtime compares price direction with open-interest expansion or contraction, classifies participation, produces structured bullish/bearish/neutral context, and never executes orders directly.

## Policy
- XM only
- GOLD# only
- Live execution disabled
- Context before decision intelligence
- No direct execution from open-interest data
