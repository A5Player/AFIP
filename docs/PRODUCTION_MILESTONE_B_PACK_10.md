# Production Milestone B Pack 10

## Decision Intelligence Layer

This pack adds financial decision intelligence components that convert fused intelligence, market context, execution quality, learning, memory, and risk context into an auditable decision output.

## Components

- Decision Consensus Engine
- Decision Confidence Model
- Decision Priority Engine
- Decision Risk Adjustment
- Decision Traceability
- Production Decision Runtime

## Compatibility

All components are additive and do not modify existing runtime behaviour.
