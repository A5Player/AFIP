# Milestone S Pack 5.9 — Research Metrics & Leaderboard

Adds an execution-neutral metrics layer over Pack 5.8 blind-forward results. It computes expectancy, win rate, profit factor, confidence interval, excursions, holding statistics, risk-adjusted scores, sample sufficiency and deterministic leaderboards.

Safety: `execution_authority = NONE`; `promotion_to_execution = PROHIBITED`. Rankings are research evidence only.
