# AFIP Gold Pro V1.0 — Milestone S Pack 5.2

## Purpose
Complete the Trade Case research lifecycle and establish a production-quality historical replay recorder without changing trading logic.

## Scope
- Pre-entry market, pattern, regime, session, multi-timeframe, intelligence and evidence fields.
- Decision trace and PASS/WAIT/BLOCK gate records with current value, threshold and reason.
- MT5 execution metadata including OrderCheck, OrderSend, latency, slippage, broker, server, symbol, digits, point and spread.
- Holding timeline for floating P/L, MFE, MAE, SL/TP movement, break-even, partial close and trailing stop.
- Exit reason, exit quality, retained profit and given-back profit.
- Post-trade observation schedule: M15, M30, H1, H4 and D1.
- Candle-by-candle replay queue, progress, metadata, event ledger and statistics.
- Permanent dashboard research section with Top 100 patterns and research-only similarity monitor.

## Safety
This pack is read-only with respect to trading. It does not change signals, thresholds, risk approval, order allocation, execution permissions or broker transmission. Optimization is disabled. V2 remains locked.
