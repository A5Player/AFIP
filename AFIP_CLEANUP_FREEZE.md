# AFIP Cleanup Freeze

Effective immediately:

1. No new Intelligence, Engine, Planner, Gateway or Feature.
2. No threshold reduction.
3. No automatic research policy activation.
4. Demo and live execution remain stopped during architecture cleanup.
5. Patch Only.
6. Backward compatibility is preserved until an owner module is certified.
7. A module may be removed only after:
   - owner selection,
   - dependency mapping,
   - adapter plan,
   - regression coverage,
   - migration record.
8. Exactly one primary authority is allowed for:
   - market regime,
   - signal,
   - confidence,
   - risk approval,
   - unit allocation,
   - position sizing,
   - protection planning,
   - execution,
   - holding,
   - exit,
   - research policy approval.
